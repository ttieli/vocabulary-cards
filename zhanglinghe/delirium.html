Delirium
/dɪˈlɪriəm/
谵妄;精神错乱
英文: A disturbed state of mind characterized by restlessness, illusions, and incoherence; an acutely confused state of consciousness.

中文: 理智与情感崩溃的混乱状态;一种精神错乱的急性意识混乱状态,带有诗意疯狂的病态美感。
"Between desire and despair, he wandered in a delirium of love."
在欲望与绝望之间,他徘徊在爱的谵妄中。
