宁安如梦 The Double
Shadow
/ˈʃædəʊ/
影子;阴影
英文: A dark area or shape produced by something blocking the light; a person or thing that follows or accompanies someone constantly.

中文: 物体遮挡光线而产生的阴暗区域;始终跟随或陪伴某人的人或事物。
"In the palace, everyone lives in the shadow of power and fate."
在宫廷中,每个人都生活在权力与命运的阴影之下。
